FEATURED ARTIST

Baraka Kamuzoga

OVERVIEW 20 QUESTIONS WORKS
Baraka is a reknown guitarist, singer and songwiter based in Nyamagana Mwanza. He is best known for his shredding skills which are most notable in his hit single Roho yangu released in May 2009. Although no one in Baraka's family has ever chosen a career in the music industry he's been heard saying, they(Everyone else in his family) are definitely better musicians than me, everything I know I learnt from them.
1

How do you come up with your wonderful melodies?

I'd say its inspiration, you'd be amazed with what the proper inspiration can do.

2

Was your hit song Saa nyingine targeted at anyone in particular?

[Laughs] This is one of those moments I wish I could plead the fifth. The answer is yes and no; it was definitely inspired by a real situation but it wasn't limited to it.
